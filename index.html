üîÑ From SaaS to RaaS Transition

Agentic AI is transforming the software industry from "selling tools" to "selling outcomes". The RaaS model enables customers to pay only for measurable KPIs.
Example: Navimod's robo-advisor service charges not for portfolio management "software", but directly based on investment returns. As AI reliability increases, this model will become widespread across all B2B SaaS segments.
